Our Rooms

“Let the spirit of the remarkable olenge grass roof soothe you into a peaceful night’s rest”

From the outside, our cottages are an architectural homage to those who came before us. Step inside and transition into the pleasures of the present day. We have different room types to meet our guests’ different needs and elevate comfort for the duration of their stay. Whether you’re a business professional, travelling solo, with family, as a couple or a group of friends having a good time, there’s something for you.

Standard features in all rooms include:

• King size bed • Work station • Coffee and tea facilities • HDTV with DSTV • Compact refrigerator • WiFi • Mesh screen windows • Natural temperature cooling • Spacious ensuite bathroom with WC and rain shower heads • Hair dryer • Courtesy kit with soap, shampoo and conditioner • Room service • Safe
